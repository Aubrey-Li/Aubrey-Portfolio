BearHub is a social platform designed to reinvent networking among alumni and college students. We identified gaps between networking needs and current solutions. For instance, student resources are often limited to a few friends and advisors when seeking course recommendations and off-campus apartments; they also have trouble finding active alumni when looking for career opportunities. At the same time, active alumni have limited opportunity to connect with most students, and there's no effective way for alumni to stay connected and updated across countries and industries among themselves.
