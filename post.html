Post-) is a modern web platform designed to help people make new friends or enhance existing relationships through common plans and goals. Making new friends or maintaining existing friendships can be hard, especially when we all have a lot to do in our life. It is even more so during the pandemic, when friendships don't thrive under remote environment. Post-) is designed as a platform for those with similar goals or future plans to connect with each other and achieve those goals and leave great memories together through collective bouding experiences. No matter if it's a plan to hit the gym every morning for a month, a weekend trip to Los Angeles, or even finding housemates in a new city, there's always those in a similar situation, and connection doesn't need to be that hard.
